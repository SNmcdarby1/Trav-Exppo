Xascapade local tours operates tailor made safari packages. Kenya's national parks are the biggest draw for any visitor. Tsavo National Park is one of the best places in Africa to see lions, elephants, giraffes and leopards. Explore the dramatic cliffs and gorges of the Rift Valley in Hell's Gate and take a spectacular balloon ride over the grasslands of the Masai Mara wildlife reserve.

When you embark on one of our self drive safaris in Africa, you are in the driver’s seat. Enjoy game driving through Africa’s premium game parks while you decide how long to spend watching wildlife. Self drive safaris in Africa is truly an unforgettable experience . We’ll provide the safari plan, the Land Rover vehicles, international vehicle paperwork and advice on what to pack. The crew prepares your meals and organizes activities; you simply drive and enjoy the scenery.

Xascapade tours organise trips for international groups from different backgrounds one our of biggest safari trucks, perfect for large group charters for up to 15 people on our of biggest safari trucks, and for large group we can charter for up to 26 people. A large group can be anything from a travel agent organising group travel to Africa, a large or extended family, a sporting group, a bunch of friends, students from a university to school trips.
